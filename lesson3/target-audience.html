Target Audience

The target audience will be people that enjoy to be outdoors and do so with other groups of people. They live an active lifestyle and range from 16-50+ both male and female. They have an average to low income meaning they are willing to spend money on gear to perform activities but are happy with simply going on walks/hikes that don't cost much money. They are able to transport themselves so they are high school education and beyond. It is open to single people with many friends as well as families. The majority of the audience will access this via mobile (ios or android) or desktop (mac/windows).
