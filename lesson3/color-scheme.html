Background

The background color of the site will be Platinum #ECE9E9. This helps keep the background less plain without it being white and allowing the other colors to appear more easily.

Navigation and Footer

The main navigation and footer will have a background color of Morning Blue #555B6E and hover color of Powder Blue #BEE3DB. These blues have a more nature tone and even the names sound inviting. In contrast, the color White will be used for text in that area. Footer links will be Apricot #FFD6BA.

Headers and Body

The headers will use the color Independence #555B6E and bolded for greater readability. The regular text in the bodies will be the color Black.
